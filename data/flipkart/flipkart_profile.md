# Flipkart

## Company Overview
Flipkart is an Indian e-commerce company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, it grew into one of India's largest online marketplaces, spanning electronics, fashion (via Myntra), groceries, and more. Walmart acquired a majority stake in Flipkart in 2018.

## Mission & Values
Flipkart's mission centres on transforming commerce in India through technology and customer-centricity. Its stated values include Audacity, Bias for Action, Customer First, and Integrity. "Audacity" reflects the founders' early ambition to build an entire e-commerce ecosystem from scratch, and the culture prizes data-driven, fearless decision-making.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Flipkart maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing with sellers and customers, data privacy, and anti-harassment, with whistle-blower mechanisms. As part of the Walmart group it aligns with broader compliance and ethics standards.

## Culture & Work Model
Flipkart's culture is described as dynamic, fast-paced, and innovative, with a strong emphasis on customer obsession, data-driven decisions, and employee empowerment. It is credited with shaping a generation of Indian startup leaders (the "Flipkart mafia"). The company operates a hybrid work model.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Flipkart runs diversity and inclusion programs across gender, disability, and LGBTQ+ inclusion, with return-to-work initiatives for women and employee resource groups. It reports on diversity and supports inclusive hiring and well-being.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
The Flipkart Foundation and related initiatives support livelihoods, skilling, and community development, and the company works on supply-chain inclusion (e.g., supporting kiranas, artisans, and MSMEs through programs like Flipkart Samarth). It has public commitments on sustainable packaging, electric mobility in delivery, and emission reduction reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Flipkart hires across experience levels, including campus and early-career programs. It offers stock options for eligible roles, health and insurance coverage, parental support, and well-being programs as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by role.

# Persistent Systems

## Company Overview
Persistent Systems is an Indian multinational software and digital engineering company headquartered in Pune, India. Founded in 1990 by Dr. Anand Deshpande, it began as an outsourced product-development company and has grown into a digital engineering and enterprise modernisation firm serving software, banking, healthcare, and other industries.

## Mission & Values
Persistent emphasises a software-product engineering heritage and a culture of gratitude, community, and continuous learning championed by its founder. Its values stress client centricity, innovation, collaboration, and giving back to society. The company positions itself as a digital engineering partner that builds and modernises software.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Persistent maintains a Code of Business Conduct and Ethics covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, intellectual-property protection, and human rights, supported by whistle-blower mechanisms.

## Culture & Work Model
Persistent's culture reflects its engineering roots, with strong emphasis on technical depth, learning, and community engagement. It operates a hybrid work model and invests in upskilling around cloud, data, and AI. Founder Anand Deshpande is known for promoting employee engagement, gratitude, and knowledge sharing.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Persistent runs diversity and inclusion programs across gender, disability, and background, with employee networks and well-being initiatives. It reports diversity metrics and supports women in technology and leadership.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Through the Persistent Foundation, the company supports health, education, disability, and community development in India. Persistent has public environmental commitments, including emission-reduction and net-zero ambitions reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Persistent hires across experience levels, including freshers and early-career engineers. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

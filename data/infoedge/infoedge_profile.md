# Info Edge (Naukri.com)

## Company Overview
Info Edge is an Indian internet company headquartered in Noida, India, best known for the recruitment platform Naukri.com. Founded in 1995 by Sanjeev Bikhchandani, Info Edge operates a portfolio of online classifieds and platforms including Naukri (jobs), 99acres (real estate), Jeevansathi (matrimony), and Shiksha (education), and is a prominent early investor in Indian startups such as Zomato and Policybazaar.

## Mission & Values
Info Edge's approach is anchored in "following the customer," frugality and fiscal discipline (from its bootstrapped origins), perseverance, trust across all relationships, and continuous innovation. Founder Sanjeev Bikhchandani is known for prioritising employees during crises—choosing to protect jobs over short-term profits.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a listed company, Info Edge maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, anti-harassment, and insider-trading prevention, with whistle-blower mechanisms and strong corporate-governance practices.

## Culture & Work Model
Info Edge's culture reflects customer focus, frugality, trust, and long-term thinking. It is known for steady, profitable internet businesses and a disciplined, founder-led ethos. The company supports office-centric and hybrid work depending on the role.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Info Edge runs diversity and inclusion initiatives across gender and background, with well-being and employee-engagement programs, and reports related efforts as part of its listed-company disclosures.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Info Edge supports education, skilling, and social-impact initiatives, and founder Sanjeev Bikhchandani is active in entrepreneurship and education philanthropy (including support for institutions like Ashoka University, which he co-founded). The company reports governance and responsible-business practices in its disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Info Edge hires across technology, sales, and corporate roles and offers stock options for eligible roles. It highlights employee protection during downturns, well-being, and learning benefits in its communications. Specific benefits vary by role.

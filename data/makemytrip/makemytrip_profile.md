# MakeMyTrip

## Company Overview
MakeMyTrip is an Indian online travel company headquartered in Gurugram, India. Founded in 2000 by Deep Kalra, it initially served the US-India diaspora travel market before expanding to domestic Indian travel. MakeMyTrip listed on the Nasdaq in 2010 and operates leading travel brands including MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, and redBus.

## Mission & Values
MakeMyTrip's purpose centres on making travel simple and enjoyable for its customers, using technology to help people plan, book, and experience trips. Its culture, shaped by the founder's early hustle, emphasises customer-centricity, perseverance, innovation, and ownership.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a Nasdaq-listed company, MakeMyTrip maintains a Code of Business Conduct and Ethics covering integrity, anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, insider-trading prevention, and equal-opportunity employment, with whistle-blower mechanisms and listed-company governance standards.

## Culture & Work Model
MakeMyTrip's culture is entrepreneurial and customer-focused, with stories of early-stage hustle (founders "slept in the office") that shaped its resilience. It emphasises innovation in travel technology and supports flexible/hybrid arrangements for many roles.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
MakeMyTrip runs diversity and inclusion initiatives across gender and background, with well-being and parental-support programs, and emphasises inclusive hiring across its travel-technology workforce.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
The MakeMyTrip Foundation supports community, disaster-relief, and responsible-tourism initiatives, and the company promotes sustainable-travel options (such as carbon-offset choices) for customers. It reports governance and responsible-business practices as a listed company.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
MakeMyTrip hires across technology, product, and business roles and offers stock options for eligible roles. It highlights health, well-being, and travel-related benefits on its careers communications. Specific benefits vary by role.

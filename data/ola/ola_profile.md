# Ola (ANI Technologies)

## Company Overview
Ola, operated by ANI Technologies, is an Indian mobility company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2010 by Bhavish Aggarwal and Ankit Bhati, it began as a ride-hailing platform and expanded into electric mobility through Ola Electric (which manufactures electric scooters and is separately listed). The founder also launched Krutrim, an AI venture.

## Mission & Values
Ola's mission centres on making urban transport more accessible, affordable, and—through Ola Electric—cleaner and more sustainable. Founder Bhavish Aggarwal emphasises excellence and results-driven culture, hiring people whose ambition and "jazba and junoon" (passion and drive) match the company's beliefs, and has publicly championed an "India-first," societal-benefit view of technology and open protocols.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Ola maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing with driver and customer partners, data privacy, and anti-harassment, with channels to report concerns. Ola Electric, as a listed entity, follows formal listed-company governance and disclosure standards.

## Culture & Work Model
Ola's culture is intense, fast-paced, and results-oriented, reflecting the founder's emphasis on excellence and ambition. The company has been associated with high-velocity execution across ride-hailing, EV manufacturing, and AI. Work arrangements are largely office-centric.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Ola has run initiatives to improve gender diversity, including programs to onboard women drivers and to build inclusive teams, and supports well-being programs. It reports diversity efforts as part of its people and (for Ola Electric) listed-company disclosures.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Sustainability is central to Ola's EV strategy: Ola Electric promotes electric two-wheelers and domestic manufacturing (including battery/cell ambitions) to reduce transport emissions. The group supports mobility, environment, and community initiatives, with ESG disclosures for its listed entity.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Ola hires across engineering, manufacturing, operations, and corporate roles. It offers stock options for eligible roles and standard health and well-being benefits as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by role and entity.

# PhonePe

## Company Overview
PhonePe is an Indian digital-payments and financial-services company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in December 2015 by Sameer Nigam, Rahul Chari, and Burzin Engineer (former Flipkart colleagues), it became one of India's largest UPI-based payments apps and has expanded into insurance, lending distribution, wealth, and other financial services.

## Mission & Values
PhonePe's mission is to make digital payments universally accessible, secure, and easy for everyone in India—bringing digital payments to the fingertips of every Indian. The company emphasises simplicity, security, accessibility, innovation, collaboration, inclusivity, and employee empowerment.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
PhonePe maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and information security, with channels to report concerns. As a regulated financial-services business, compliance and security are central.

## Culture & Work Model
PhonePe fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration with a large engineering organisation, emphasising inclusivity and empowerment. The company has communicated employee-friendly approaches and supports both office-centric and hybrid arrangements depending on the role.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
PhonePe runs diversity and inclusion initiatives across gender and background, with well-being and parental-support programs, and emphasises inclusive hiring. It has publicly highlighted ESOP buyback events that benefited a broad set of employees.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
PhonePe supports financial inclusion and digital-literacy efforts aligned with its mission of universal digital payments, and participates in community and disaster-relief initiatives. As a private company it follows responsible-business practices.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
PhonePe hires across engineering, product, and business roles and offers stock options (ESOPs) for eligible roles, with periodic ESOP liquidity events publicly reported. It highlights insurance, parental, and well-being benefits on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by role.

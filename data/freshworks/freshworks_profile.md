# Freshworks

## Company Overview
Freshworks is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) company founded in Chennai, India, in 2010 by Girish Mathrubootham and Shan Krishnasamy. It builds customer-service, IT, sales, and marketing software designed to be easy to use and quick to deploy. In September 2021 it became the first Indian SaaS company to list on the Nasdaq (ticker FRSH).

## Mission & Values
Freshworks emphasises making business software easy and delightful, with a stated focus on customer happiness over aggressive sales targets and pride in "building from India for the world." Its values centre on empathy, customer-centricity, and a heart-led, employee-first approach to building products and teams.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a Nasdaq-listed company, Freshworks maintains a Code of Business Conduct and Ethics covering integrity, anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, insider-trading prevention, and equal-opportunity employment, with whistle-blower mechanisms and listed-company governance standards.

## Culture & Work Model
Freshworks is known for its "kudumba" (family/home) culture—a friendly, open environment with strong opportunities for young talent and open communication. The company encourages employees to learn, experiment, and grow, and is credited with making hundreds of employees shareholders through stock options at its IPO. It operates a hybrid work model.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Freshworks promotes inclusion across gender and background, with employee resource groups and well-being programs. As a global company built from Chennai, it emphasises opportunity for talent regardless of pedigree and supports women's representation in tech roles.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Freshworks supports education, skilling, and community programs, and founder Girish Mathrubootham is active in philanthropy and the Indian startup ecosystem (including initiatives like SaaSBOOMi). As a listed company it reports on governance and responsible-business practices.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Freshworks hires across experience levels and is known for hiring and growing young talent. It offers stock options (ESOPs), learning opportunities, health and well-being benefits, and a collaborative environment as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

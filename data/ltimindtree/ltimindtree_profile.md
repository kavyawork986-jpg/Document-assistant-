# LTIMindtree

## Company Overview
LTIMindtree is an Indian multinational IT services and digital transformation company headquartered in Mumbai/Bengaluru, India. It was formed in November 2022 through the merger of Larsen & Toubro Infotech (LTI) and Mindtree, and is part of the L&T Group. The company provides digital, cloud, data, and consulting services to global enterprises.

## Mission & Values
LTIMindtree positions itself around solving complex business challenges and enabling enterprise transformation at scale. Its culture blends L&T Infotech's engineering rigour with Mindtree's people-centric, innovation-led ethos. The company emphasises customer-centricity, collaboration, and responsible business, drawing on the L&T Group's reputation for integrity and project excellence.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As part of the L&T Group, LTIMindtree follows a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data protection, and human rights, supported by whistle-blower mechanisms. Strong corporate governance is inherited from the L&T parent.

## Culture & Work Model
The merged company combines two distinct cultures—LTI's structured delivery heritage and Mindtree's flatter, collaborative style—into a unified organisation. It operates a hybrid work model and invests in reskilling across cloud, data, and AI. Mindtree's legacy of community and "Mindtree Minds" identity continues to influence the people culture.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
LTIMindtree runs diversity and inclusion programs across gender, disability, and nationality, with employee resource groups and well-being support. It reports diversity metrics and supports women's representation and career progression.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
LTIMindtree invests in education, skilling, health, and environmental programs through its CSR initiatives, building on the philanthropic traditions of both legacy companies. It has public environmental commitments, including emission-reduction and net-zero ambitions reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
LTIMindtree hires across experience levels, including freshers via campus programs. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

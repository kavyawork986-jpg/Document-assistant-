# Meesho

## Company Overview
Meesho is an Indian e-commerce company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2015 by Vidit Aatrey and Sanjeev Barnwal (IIT Delhi hostel-mates), it began by enabling social-commerce resellers and grew into a value-focused online marketplace known for low-cost products and a zero/low-commission model for small sellers, especially serving price-sensitive "Bharat" consumers.

## Mission & Values
Meesho's mission is to democratise internet commerce for everyone, enabling small businesses and individual entrepreneurs—with a vision to help 100 million small businesses succeed online. Customer and user obsession is central: leadership has made it mandatory for everyone to regularly speak with the community of entrepreneurs and users so their needs stay front of mind.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Meesho maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing with sellers and customers, data privacy, and anti-harassment, with channels to report concerns.

## Culture & Work Model
Meesho's culture emphasises customer obsession, frugality, and high ownership, reflecting its mission to serve small sellers and value-conscious consumers. It runs a monthly user-townhall series ("WeHearYou") to gather candid feedback. Meesho is notable for progressive people policies and has experimented with flexible and remote-friendly work.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Meesho has publicly introduced inclusive people policies, including gender-neutral and extended parental-leave approaches, support for new parents, and well-being programs. It promotes diversity across gender and background and reports related efforts.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Meesho's core social impact is economic inclusion—enabling small sellers, resellers, and home-based entrepreneurs (many of them women) to earn online with little capital. It supports skilling and seller-enablement initiatives aligned with its democratisation mission.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Meesho hires across engineering, product, and business roles and offers stock options (ESOPs) for eligible roles. It has publicly highlighted progressive benefits such as gender-neutral parental leave, well-being support, and flexible work, including a "reset and recharge" company-wide break in the past. Specific benefits vary by role.

# Wipro

## Company Overview
Wipro is an Indian multinational IT services, consulting, and business-process services company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. With roots going back to 1945 as a vegetable-oil business (Western India Products), it transformed under chairman Azim Premji into a leading global technology services firm. Today Wipro provides digital, cloud, engineering, and consulting services worldwide.

## Mission & Values
Wipro's value system is anchored in the "Spirit of Wipro," described by the company as its beacon and sense of purpose. It encompasses being passionate about clients' success, treating each person with respect, being globally and ecologically responsible, and unyielding integrity in everything. In 2020 the company introduced "Five Habits" to drive a growth mindset: Being respectful, Being responsive, Always communicating, Demonstrating stewardship, and Building trust.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Wipro's Code of Business Conduct (COBC) is grounded in the Spirit of Wipro and sets ethical guidelines on integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair competition, data protection, and human rights. The company emphasises "unyielding integrity" as a non-negotiable value and provides ombuds and whistle-blower channels.

## Culture & Work Model
Wipro is known for a values-led, governance-focused culture shaped by Azim Premji's emphasis on ethics and philanthropy. It operates a hybrid work model and invests in large-scale reskilling. The Five Habits framework is used to reinforce collaboration, accountability, and trust across teams.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Wipro promotes inclusion across gender, disability, and nationality, with employee resource groups and structured diversity goals. It publishes diversity metrics and runs programs to increase women's participation and support people with disabilities, reflected in its sustainability disclosures.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Azim Premji is among the world's most prominent philanthropists; a large share of his Wipro holdings is committed to the Azim Premji Foundation, which works extensively in school education and public systems in India. Wipro has long-standing environmental commitments, including energy, water, and emission goals reported in its sustainability reports, and was an early adopter of corporate sustainability reporting in India.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Wipro hires at scale, including freshers through campus and its work-integrated learning programs. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

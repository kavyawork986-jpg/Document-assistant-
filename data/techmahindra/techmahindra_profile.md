# Tech Mahindra

## Company Overview
Tech Mahindra is an Indian multinational IT services and consulting company headquartered in Pune, India, and is part of the Mahindra Group. It provides digital transformation, consulting, networks, and engineering services, with particular strength in telecom and communications. The company is known for its "Connected World, Connected Experiences" positioning.

## Mission & Values
Tech Mahindra is guided by the Mahindra Group's "Rise" purpose: to challenge conventional thinking and use resources innovatively to drive positive change and enable communities to Rise. Its three Rise pillars are Accepting No Limits, Alternative Thinking, and Driving Positive Change. Core values include Good Corporate Citizenship, Professionalism, Customer First, Quality Focus, and Dignity of the Individual.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Tech Mahindra follows a Code of Conduct/Ethics aligned with the Mahindra Group, covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data protection, and human rights. It upholds the individual's right to express disagreement and respects others' time and effort, with whistle-blower channels for reporting concerns.

## Culture & Work Model
Tech Mahindra's culture emphasises purpose, sustainability, and a "Rise for Good" orientation. It celebrates individual dignity and fairness, and operates a hybrid work model. The company invests in reskilling for next-generation technologies such as 5G, AI, and cloud.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
The company runs diversity and inclusion programs across gender, disability, and nationality, with employee networks and well-being support. It reports diversity metrics and supports women in technology and leadership.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Through the Tech Mahindra Foundation, the company focuses on education, employability, and disability empowerment in India, including its Skill for Market (SMART) employability centres. Tech Mahindra has public environmental commitments, including carbon-reduction and net-zero goals reported in its sustainability disclosures, consistent with the Mahindra Group's "Rise for Good" sustainability focus.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Tech Mahindra hires across experience levels, including freshers and early-career programs. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

# Nykaa (FSN E-Commerce Ventures)

## Company Overview
Nykaa, operated by FSN E-Commerce Ventures, is an Indian beauty and lifestyle e-commerce company headquartered in Mumbai, India. Founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, a former investment banker, it became a leading omnichannel retailer of beauty, personal-care, and fashion products and listed publicly in India in 2021.

## Mission & Values
Nykaa's mission is to create a world where consumers have access to a finely curated, authentic assortment of products and services that "delight and elevate the human spirit." A central value is to be "bold but good"—empowering employees to dream big while doing the right things the right way, without shortcuts. Trust, authenticity, customer experience, and long-term, purpose-driven thinking are core.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a listed company, Nykaa maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, product authenticity, data privacy, anti-harassment, and insider-trading prevention, with whistle-blower mechanisms.

## Culture & Work Model
Nykaa's culture emphasises authenticity, customer experience, and empowerment—teams have the freedom to experiment and run with ideas. Founder Falguni Nayar models long-term, values-led leadership. The company operates across corporate, retail, and warehouse functions with arrangements suited to each.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Nykaa is notable for strong representation of women, including in leadership, and founder Falguni Nayar actively advocates for women's development and leadership. The company promotes a diverse, inclusive work environment and reports related efforts.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Nykaa supports women's empowerment, skilling, and community initiatives, and works on responsible-retail practices including sustainable and clean-beauty assortments. It reports governance and responsible-business practices in its disclosures as a listed company.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Nykaa hires across technology, retail, content, and corporate roles. It offers stock options for eligible roles and highlights well-being, parental, and inclusive workplace benefits on its careers communications. Specific benefits vary by role.

# HCLTech

## Company Overview
HCLTech (HCL Technologies) is an Indian multinational IT services and consulting company headquartered in Noida, India. Part of the HCL enterprise founded by Shiv Nadar, HCLTech provides digital, engineering, cloud, and software services to clients worldwide. Its positioning line is "Supercharging Progress."

## Mission & Values
HCLTech's most distinctive philosophy is "Employees First, Customers Second" (EFCS), the belief that empowered employees in the customer-facing "value zone" are the primary drivers of value. Its stated values include Ideapreneurship, Trust and Transparency, Value Centricity, and Flexibility. Ideapreneurship is the company's grassroots innovation culture that encourages employees at all levels to generate and act on ideas.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
HCLTech maintains a Code of Business Ethics and Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and human rights. It provides whistle-blower and ombudsperson mechanisms and emphasises trust and transparency as core values.

## Culture & Work Model
HCLTech's culture is shaped by the Ideapreneurship movement and the EFCS philosophy of "inverting the organizational pyramid" so that management enables frontline employees. Programs such as its idea/value portal have historically collected tens of thousands of employee ideas. The company operates a hybrid work model and invests in large-scale digital skilling, including its TechBee early-careers program for school graduates.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
HCLTech runs structured diversity and inclusion programs across gender, generations, disability, and nationality, with employee networks and well-being initiatives. It reports diversity metrics and supports women's career growth and return-to-work pathways.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Through the HCL Foundation, the company invests in rural development, education, health, environment, and disaster response in India. HCLTech has public environmental commitments, including net-zero and renewable-energy goals reported in its sustainability disclosures. Shiv Nadar is also a major philanthropist in education through the Shiv Nadar Foundation.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
HCLTech hires across experience levels and offers an early-careers path (TechBee) for students after Grade 12. It provides learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

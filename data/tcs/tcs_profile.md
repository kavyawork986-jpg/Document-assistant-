# Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

## Company Overview
Tata Consultancy Services is an Indian multinational IT services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai, India. Founded in 1968, it is part of the Tata Group and is one of the largest IT services firms in the world by market capitalisation and employee count. TCS operates across the globe, offering consulting, software, business process services, and digital transformation. Its brand line is "Building on Belief."

## Mission & Values
TCS's purpose is to use technology to help customers, communities, and the world at large grow and transform. As a Tata Group company, it inherits the Tata values: Integrity, Responsibility, Excellence, Pioneering, and Unity. The company emphasises long-term relationships, customer-centricity, and creating greater value for stakeholders through belief in the potential of its people and technology.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
TCS follows the Tata Code of Conduct (TCoC), the ethical framework that applies across all Tata Group companies. It sets expectations on integrity, fair dealing, avoidance of conflicts of interest, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, protection of confidential information, and respect for human rights. The code applies to employees, directors, and, in relevant ways, to business partners. A whistle-blower mechanism allows confidential reporting of violations.

## Culture & Work Model
TCS is known for structured, scale-driven delivery and a strong learning culture. It has historically favoured on-site/office-based delivery and has publicly stated a preference for employees working from office to strengthen collaboration and culture, while operating a global network delivery model. Internal mobility, certifications, and continuous reskilling are central to its talent approach.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
TCS employs a very large, multinational workforce spanning many nationalities. It runs programs to increase women's representation in the workforce and in leadership, supports return-to-work initiatives, and promotes an inclusive workplace. Diversity, equity, and inclusion are reported in its annual and sustainability disclosures.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
TCS invests in education, skilling, employability, health, and digital inclusion through flagship initiatives such as its digital literacy and IT skilling programs. It has public commitments on environmental sustainability, including emission-reduction targets and net-zero ambitions reported in its ESG disclosures, consistent with the Tata Group's community focus.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
TCS hires at large scale, including significant fresher recruitment from campuses across India and structured early-career programs. It provides learning platforms and certification pathways, health and insurance coverage, and employee assistance and well-being programs as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefit details vary by country and role.

# Mphasis

## Company Overview
Mphasis is an Indian multinational IT services and consulting company headquartered in Bengaluru, India (with a significant US presence). It specialises in cloud and cognitive services, application development, and digital transformation, with deep expertise in banking, financial services, and insurance. The company describes itself as a "Hi-Tech, Hi-Touch, Hi-Trust" organisation.

## Mission & Values
Mphasis centres its strategy on customer-centricity, expressed through its proprietary Front2Back transformation approach, which uses cloud and cognitive technology to deliver hyper-personalised digital experiences. Its cultural DNA emphasises imagination, innovation, and the willingness to take risks, anchored by trust with clients and employees.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Mphasis maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and human rights, with whistle-blower mechanisms for reporting concerns. Strong governance and information-security practices are emphasised given its financial-services client base.

## Culture & Work Model
Mphasis fosters a learning-and-growth culture built on its Hi-Tech, Hi-Touch, Hi-Trust identity. It operates a hybrid work model and invests in reskilling around cloud, AI, and next-generation technologies. Customer relationships and applied technology are central to how teams work.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Mphasis runs diversity and inclusion programs across gender, disability, and background, with employee networks and well-being initiatives. It reports diversity efforts and supports women's representation and accessibility in the workplace.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
The Mphasis F1 Foundation focuses on inclusion of persons with disabilities, skilling, and community development in India. Mphasis has public environmental commitments, including emission-reduction and net-zero ambitions reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Mphasis hires across experience levels, including early-career programs. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

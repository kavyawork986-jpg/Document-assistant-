# Coforge

## Company Overview
Coforge (formerly NIIT Technologies) is an Indian multinational IT services and digital solutions company headquartered in Noida, India. Founded in 1992 as NIIT Technologies and rebranded to Coforge in 2020, it focuses on selected industry verticals such as banking and financial services, insurance, and travel, transportation, and hospitality.

## Mission & Values
Coforge positions itself around deep domain expertise and an "engaging, dependable" partnership style, emphasising client intimacy in a focused set of industries. Its stated approach values integrity, customer obsession, execution excellence, and innovation in emerging technologies such as AI, cloud, and data.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Coforge maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and human rights, supported by whistle-blower mechanisms. Governance and information security are emphasised given its regulated-industry clients.

## Culture & Work Model
Coforge fosters a culture of domain specialisation and execution, operating a hybrid work model and investing in reskilling around AI, cloud, and automation. Its focused-vertical strategy shapes how teams build expertise and client relationships.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Coforge runs diversity and inclusion efforts across gender, disability, and background, with well-being and employee-engagement initiatives. It reports on diversity and supports women's participation in the workforce.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Coforge invests in education, skilling, health, and environmental programs through its CSR initiatives. It has public sustainability commitments, including emission-reduction goals reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Coforge hires across experience levels, including freshers and early-career hires. It offers learning platforms, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by country and role.

# Razorpay

## Company Overview
Razorpay is an Indian fintech company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2014 by Harshil Mathur and Shashank Kumar (both IIT Roorkee graduates), it provides payment-gateway, business-banking, and financial-infrastructure products for businesses. Razorpay is a leading payments platform for Indian online businesses.

## Mission & Values
Razorpay's core premise is to make money movement simpler and easier—receiving, sending, and managing money—for Indian businesses. It is known for a deep product focus and an engineering-first culture. Cultural principles emphasised by its founders include transparency, innovation through failure ("if you are not failing, you are not innovating enough"), and a high-trust, owner-like environment.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Razorpay maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and information security, with channels to report concerns. As a regulated payments business, compliance, security, and customer-data protection are emphasised.

## Culture & Work Model
Razorpay's culture is engineering- and product-led, with strong transparency (open sharing of performance and decisions) and psychological safety that encourages experimentation. Its hiring philosophy favours candidates with a "high slope" (rate of learning and potential) over only current credentials. The company supports flexible/hybrid work for many roles.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Razorpay runs diversity and inclusion initiatives across gender and background, with well-being and parental-support programs, and emphasises inclusive hiring and a high-trust environment.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Razorpay supports the startup and small-business ecosystem and financial enablement of businesses, and participates in skilling and community initiatives. As a private company it follows responsible-business practices rather than the formal ESG-report format of large listed firms.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Razorpay hires across engineering, product, and business roles, and offers stock options (ESOPs) for eligible roles. It publicly highlights benefits such as comprehensive insurance, parental and well-being support, and flexible work as described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by role.

# Paytm (One97 Communications)

## Company Overview
Paytm is the consumer brand of One97 Communications Limited, an Indian fintech company headquartered in Noida, India. One97 was founded in 2000 by Vijay Shekhar Sharma, and the Paytm payments brand launched in 2010. The company offers digital payments, merchant services, and financial-services partnerships, and is publicly listed in India.

## Mission & Values
Paytm's stated mission is to bring half a billion Indians into the mainstream economy through digital payments and financial inclusion. Its strategy combines payments scale with a broader "payments + financial services" platform spanning lending, insurance, wealth, and commerce. The company emphasises trust, customer support, and enabling India's digital-economy journey.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a listed company, One97/Paytm maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, insider-trading prevention, and regulatory compliance, with whistle-blower mechanisms. Given its regulated fintech operations, compliance and information security are emphasised.

## Culture & Work Model
Paytm's culture is entrepreneurial and fast-paced, shaped by founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma's mission-driven, customer-trust orientation. It emphasises innovation in payments technology and a large merchant ecosystem. The company operates from office-centric and hybrid arrangements.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Paytm runs diversity and inclusion efforts across gender and background, with well-being and employee-engagement initiatives, and reports on diversity as part of its listed-company disclosures.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Paytm supports financial literacy, digital inclusion, education, and disaster-relief initiatives through its CSR programs, aligned with its financial-inclusion mission. It reports governance and responsible-business practices in its annual and ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Paytm hires across experience levels, including engineering and early-career roles. It offers stock options for eligible roles, health and insurance coverage, and well-being support as publicly described on its careers pages. Specific benefits vary by role.

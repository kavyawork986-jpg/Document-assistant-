# InMobi

## Company Overview
InMobi is an Indian mobile-advertising and marketing-technology company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2007 by Naveen Tewari (with co-founders), it grew into a global mobile-advertising platform and was among India's early unicorns. Its portfolio has included the InMobi advertising platform and Glance (a lock-screen content platform).

## Mission & Values
InMobi is driven by a philosophy of constant innovation and disruption, which founder Naveen Tewari describes as embedded in the DNA of "InMobians." He believes culture should permeate everything an organisation is, does, and stands for. The company is known for a high-trust, ownership-driven environment.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
InMobi maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, and advertising/data-handling standards, with channels to report concerns. Given its data-driven advertising business, privacy and responsible-data practices are emphasised.

## Culture & Work Model
InMobi is notable for an unconventional, trust-based culture. The company articulated an open-source organisational philosophy ("YaWiO" — You and We are InMobi/Owners) and has experimented with high-trust policies—such as minimal bureaucracy around leave and work hours—built on trusting employees to act as owners. It supports flexible work for many roles.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
InMobi runs inclusion and well-being initiatives across gender and background and emphasises a trust-based, ownership-driven environment. It reports people initiatives as a private company.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
As a private company, InMobi follows responsible-business practices and engages in community, skilling, and ecosystem initiatives rather than publishing the formal ESG-report format of large listed firms. Responsible handling of consumer data is central to its business ethics.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
InMobi hires across engineering, product, design, and business roles and offers stock options (ESOPs) for eligible roles. It highlights a high-trust culture, flexible policies, and well-being benefits on its careers communications. Specific benefits vary by role.

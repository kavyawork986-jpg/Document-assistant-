# GitLab People Group

## Overview

The People Group at GitLab supports team members throughout their entire employment lifecycle. The mission is to build a high-performance and results-driven culture where a diverse and inclusive team can experience a meaningful career.

## Key Contact Methods

### General Questions
- Create an issue in the People Group project on GitLab
- Use HelpLab for directed support
- Tag @gl-people-ops-team for routing to the right person

### Emergencies
- Submit a case through HelpLab (monitored during business hours)
- Contact emergency services directly for medical or critical situations

### Reporting Violations
- **EthicsPoint** — anonymous ethics hotline
- **Lighthouse Services** — 833-480-0010 (English, US/Canada) or reports@lighthouse-services.com
- **Team Member Relations** — teammemberrelations@gitlab.com
- Harassment Complaint Form available for formal documentation

## Teams Within the People Group

| Team | Focus |
|------|-------|
| People Business Partners | Strategic HR support by division |
| Total Rewards | Compensation, benefits, and equity |
| People Operations | Day-to-day HR operations |
| Talent Acquisition | Recruiting and hiring |
| Learning & Development | Training and career growth |
| Diversity, Inclusion & Belonging | DEI programs and initiatives |
| Team Member Relations | Employee relations and conflict resolution |

## Response Timelines

People Operations handles critical functions with defined turnaround times:
- **Letters of Employment** — 24 hours
- **Offboarding (voluntary)** — 24 hours
- **Organizational changes** — 3 business days
- **HelpLab queries** — 24 hours

## Employment Lifecycle Support

The People Group supports team members at every stage:
- **Hiring** — Talent Acquisition partners with hiring managers to find and onboard the right people
- **Onboarding** — Structured onboarding ensures new hires are set up for success from day one
- **Development** — Learning & Development provides resources for career growth
- **Performance** — People Business Partners support managers with performance conversations
- **Offboarding** — Respectful and compliant offboarding processes for all departures

## People Manager Calendar

GitLab maintains a People Manager Calendar (theloop.gitlab.com) to help managers stay on top of key HR deadlines, review cycles, and people processes throughout the year.

# GitLab Benefits

## Philosophy

GitLab's approach to benefits is globally aligned and locally relevant. The company adapts offerings to specific countries while ensuring core coverage is available everywhere. Core coverage includes medical, life insurance, and pension support.

GitLab's guiding principle: create programs with broad reach, high value, and long-term scalability. Benefits are designed to support team members across all life stages.

## Guiding Principles

GitLab integrates its CREDIT values into benefits design:
- **Collaboration** — Benefits support team members working together across time zones
- **Results** — Benefits remove friction so team members can focus on outcomes
- **Efficiency** — Programs are designed to be easy to understand and use
- **Diversity, Inclusion & Belonging** — Benefits are designed to be inclusive of different needs, backgrounds, and life situations
- **Iteration** — Everything at GitLab is always in draft, including benefits. Continuous improvement is expected
- **Transparency** — Benefits information is documented in the handbook and accessible to all team members

## Key Benefit Areas

### Medical Coverage
Core medical coverage is available to team members in all countries where GitLab operates, adapted to local standards and legal requirements.

### Parental Leave
GitLab provides parental leave support for all parents. A dedicated parental leave toolkit is available for both team members and managers to navigate the process.

### Pension and Retirement
Pension or retirement savings support is provided in all operating countries, aligned with local regulations and norms.

### Equity
Restricted Stock Units are part of the total rewards package, connecting team members to GitLab's long-term growth.

## Continuous Improvement Process

GitLab follows a seven-step annual cycle to evolve its benefits:
1. Listen to employee feedback
2. Research competitive benchmarks
3. Plan improvements
4. Explore options including multinational pooling
5. Make informed decisions
6. Keep teams informed about upcoming changes
7. Announce enhancements

## Getting Help

Team members can access benefits support through:
- The benefits handbook (country-specific details available)
- HelpLab — for personalized questions directed to the Total Rewards team
- People Business Partners

## Total Rewards Team

The Total Rewards team oversees compensation, benefits, and equity. They can be reached through HelpLab for any questions about pay, benefits enrollment, or equity grants.

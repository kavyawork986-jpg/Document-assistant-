# Dream11 (Dream Sports)

## Company Overview
Dream11 is an Indian fantasy-sports platform, the flagship brand of Dream Sports, headquartered in Mumbai, India. Co-founded in 2008 by Harsh Jain and Bhavit Sheth, Dream11 lets users create fantasy teams across cricket, football, and other sports. Dream Sports is a sports-technology group whose portfolio has included Dream11, FanCode, DreamSetGo, and the Dream Sports Foundation.

## Mission & Values
Dream Sports' mission is to "Make Sports Better." The company is a strong believer in building culture-driven organisations and a user-first approach to problem-solving. Harsh Jain has publicly described his role using the title "Culture Enforcement Officer," underscoring how central culture and user focus are to the business.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
Dream Sports maintains internal codes governing integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing, data privacy, responsible-gaming practices, and anti-harassment, with channels to report concerns. Given the regulated nature of online gaming in India, compliance and responsible-play safeguards are emphasised.

## Culture & Work Model
Dream Sports is known for a culture-first, user-obsessed environment with strong emphasis on sports fandom, technology, and ownership. The company invests in engineering scale to handle very large concurrent user loads during major sporting events. Work arrangements are largely office-centric with flexibility for some roles.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Dream Sports runs inclusion and well-being initiatives across gender and background and emphasises a culture-driven, merit-based environment. It reports people initiatives as a private company.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Through the Dream Sports Foundation, the company supports athletes and promotes sports development and grassroots participation in India. It engages in community and sports-ecosystem initiatives aligned with its "Make Sports Better" mission.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Dream Sports hires across engineering, product, design, and business roles and offers stock options (ESOPs) for eligible roles. It highlights sports-centric culture, learning, and well-being benefits on its careers communications. Specific benefits vary by role.

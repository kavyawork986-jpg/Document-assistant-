# Swiggy

## Company Overview
Swiggy is an Indian on-demand delivery and food-tech company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2014 by Sriharsha Majety, Nandan Reddy, and Rahul Jaimini, it grew from food delivery into a broader convenience platform including Instamart (quick commerce) and other services. Swiggy listed publicly in India in 2024.

## Mission & Values
Swiggy's mission is to elevate the quality of life for urban consumers by offering unparalleled convenience. Its culture emphasises innovation ("a huge, crazy love for tinkering"), an ownership mindset where employees are encouraged to "think like founders," learning from failure, and customer-focused, collaborative leadership.

## Code of Conduct / Ethics
As a listed company, Swiggy maintains a Code of Conduct covering integrity, anti-bribery and anti-corruption, conflicts of interest, fair dealing with restaurant and delivery partners, data privacy, anti-harassment, and insider-trading prevention, with whistle-blower mechanisms.

## Culture & Work Model
Swiggy is known for an innovation-led, experiment-friendly culture with strong ownership and a bias for action. It treats failure as a learning opportunity and emphasises metrics and individual impact. The company supports flexible/hybrid work for many corporate roles.

## Diversity, Equity & Inclusion
Swiggy has publicly introduced inclusive people policies and programs aimed at improving gender diversity, including initiatives to bring more women into delivery and corporate roles, gender-neutral parental leave, and well-being support. It reports on diversity and inclusion efforts.

## CSR / Sustainability (ESG)
Swiggy supports delivery-partner welfare, skilling, and community initiatives, and has public commitments around sustainable packaging and electric-vehicle adoption in deliveries to reduce its environmental footprint, reported in its ESG disclosures.

## Careers & Benefits (as publicly stated)
Swiggy hires across technology, operations, and corporate roles. It offers stock options for eligible roles, and has publicly highlighted progressive policies such as gender-neutral parental leave and well-being benefits. Specific benefits vary by role.
